About Us
Trend Mosaic Media (TMM) is more than just a digital platform; we’re on a mission to solve youth unemployment in Kashmir and across India. We're passionate about connecting skilled individuals like writers, designers, developers, and creatives with international companies that need top-quality freelance talent.
Our Mission
Our mission is simple: We want to create opportunities for talented youth in Kashmir and India to work on global projects and connect them with companies that need affordable, high-quality work. Through our group-based project matching system, we’re making teamwork and collaboration a reality.
